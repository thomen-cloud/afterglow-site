Afterglow

Your recovery, documented day by day.

A private journal for cosmetic-procedure recovery — one photo a day, your symptoms in your own words, and a clean report to bring to your provider.

What it does

Guided daily photos

A steady photo routine with consistent framing and lighting cues, so Day 1 and Day 12 are actually comparable.

Compare, side by side

Put any two days next to each other and see the change with your own eyes.

Day-by-day recovery guides

Reference guidance condensed from medical-society aftercare publications and peer-reviewed research — what many recovery timelines describe, day by day.

A provider-ready pack

One tap builds a clean report of your symptom log and questions, plus your photos as day-labeled files — easy to hand to your own provider.

Optional AI Photo Notes

If you choose, a short, non-diagnostic written note about what a photo appears to show. Journaling context — never a diagnosis, never medical advice.

Private by design

No account. No tracking. Your photos stay on your phone unless you choose an AI Photo Note — and then only that photo is sent, once, with location data stripped. Read the full privacy policy.

Afterglow is a personal documentation tool. It does not diagnose, treat, or monitor any condition, and it is not a substitute for your provider's instructions. Everyone heals differently — your provider's plan always comes first.
