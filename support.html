Common questions

Where are my photos stored?

On your phone. There is no account and no cloud copy by us. Your photos stay on your phone unless you choose an AI Photo Note.

How do I delete my data?

Settings → "Delete all my data" removes everything from the phone permanently: photos, logs, notes, reports, and settings.

Is the AI note medical advice?

No. It is a short, non-diagnostic description of what a photo appears to show — journaling context. Your provider's instructions always come first.

Purchases and refunds

Purchases are handled by Apple. You can restore purchases from Settings in the app; refunds go through Apple's standard process at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Accessibility

We want this site and the Afterglow app to be usable by everyone. The app supports VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, and standard iOS accessibility features, and this site is built to WCAG 2.1 AA. If anything here is difficult for you to access, email support@yourafterglow.app and we will fix it or provide the information in a format that works for you.
